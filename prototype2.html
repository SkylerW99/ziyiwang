Prototype 2

I use prototype 2 as the testing for the concept feasibility. I bought some steel wires and began morph them into the geometric shapes that assemble the abstract shape of the human silhouette. Then I made a form board box that holds the hanging steel wire. I tried to separate the shapes into different layers to create a perspective effect.

The steel wire installation part:

Steel Wire Art Reference

I went to MOMA to see the kinetic art exhibition that features Alexander Calder's steel wire installations. I get inspired about his material usage, kinetic movement, and minimalistic aesthetics.

Feedbacks

I found it's hard for the viewers to associate the front installation with the projection at the back because I failed to execute the shape and position of the steel wire precisely. I learnt I need to focus on the visual execution for the final output.
